Why Scripture Scribbles Exists

The Problem

I love digital notes. They're always with me, easy to edit, and incredibly flexible. But when I looked for Bible study apps, nothing really met my needs.

The app store is full of options, but they all fell short in one way or another:

  • Most are cluttered and overwhelming
  • Some have beautiful reading experiences but weak note-taking
  • Others have powerful annotations but poor reading design
  • None of them handled sermon notes well

So I decided to build something better.
